Soy Sauce Chicken

Soy Sauce Chicken is a favorite dish loved by many, with various ways to prepare it. The key to a delicious Soy Sauce Chicken is having a thick, creamy, sweet and spicy sauce.

Ingredients:

  • ½ chicken
  • Salt
  • Turmeric
  • 1 bowl of oil
  • 2 tbsp chili paste
  • 3 tbsp tomato sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • Sweet soy sauce
  • Red chili
  • A little water
  • 2 tbsp tamarind water
  • 1 onion, sliced into rings

Finely Pounded Ingredients:

  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 4 shallots
  • 2 inches of ginger

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken with salt and turmeric.
  2. Heat oil and fry the chicken until half-cooked. Remove and set aside.
  3. Reduce the oil in the pan, leaving just enough for sautéing. Add the pounded ingredients and stir until golden.
  4. Add chili paste, oyster sauce, tomato sauce, and the chicken. Stir briefly.
  5. Add sweet soy sauce (adjust to taste for the amount of sauce), red chili, and a little water.
  6. Cover and let the chicken cook. Then, add tomatoes and onions. Stir for a short while until the onions are slightly wilted, then turn off the heat.
  7. Serve the dish garnished with chili and spring onions.
Tip:

Use more oil for sautéing as the key to delicious Soy Sauce Chicken is having a rich and oily sauce. You can remove the excess oil after cooking.
