Spicy Coconut Chicken Recipe

Spicy Coconut Chicken is a flavorful Malaysian dish with a rich, creamy, and spicy coconut-based gravy. The aroma of turmeric leaves and fresh herbs enhances the taste, making it irresistible.

Ingredients:

  • Â½ chicken, cut into small pieces
  • 15-25 bird's eye chilies
  • 1 turmeric leaf
  • 2 pieces of fresh turmeric
  • 1 stalk of lemongrass, bruised
  • 1 piece of dried tamarind (asam gelugor)
  • 1 large bowl of coconut milk
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Clean the chicken and set it aside.
  2. Heat a pan over low heat. Add the chicken without oil and cook until the water released by the chicken dries up. This step helps remove any unpleasant smell from the chicken.
  3. Add the finely pounded bird's eye chilies and fresh turmeric into the pan with the chicken. Stir well for a few minutes, then remove the chicken and set it aside.
  4. In the same pan, pour the coconut milk and add the lemongrass. Cook over low heat until it boils.
  5. Add the chicken back into the pan along with the turmeric leaf and dried tamarind.
  6. Keep stirring to prevent the coconut milk from curdling. Add water if the gravy becomes too thick.
  7. Remove from heat and garnish with sliced turmeric leaves before serving.
Tip:

For better taste, use fresh coconut milk. If using boxed coconut milk, adjust the amount of water added to achieve the desired consistency.
